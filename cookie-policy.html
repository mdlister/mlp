1 What Are Cookies?

Simple Explanation

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to remember your preferences, login details, and other information that makes your browsing experience smoother.

Like a digital memory for websites

On Our Wedding Site

We use cookies to understand how couples like you use our site, which helps us improve our Mike Lister photography services and create better content for future brides and grooms.
